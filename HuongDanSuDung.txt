================================================================================
HUONG DAN SU DUNG - RAG Answer Faithfulness Filtering
================================================================================

Muc tieu: loc cau tra loi RAG theo do trung thuc (faithfulness) voi context.
Pipeline: retrieve -> generate -> filter(context, answer) -> accept/reject.

1. CAU TRUC CHINH
-----------------
  src/filtering/     : thu vien loc (DeBERTa, NLI, RAGAS, LLM-judge)
  configs/           : cau hinh thi nghiem
  scripts/           : chay khong can notebook
  notebooks/         : notebook thuc nghiem (0..5)
  data/              : labeled_merged.csv + labeled_merged_test.csv
  docs/              : tai lieu bo sung (tieng Anh)

2. CHAY NHANH - BASELINE DeBERTa / NLI (notebook 5)
---------------------------------------------------
Kich hoat venv, roi:

    python scripts/run_deberta_nli_baseline.py ^
      --config configs/experiments/filter_training.yaml

(Linux/macOS dung \ thay cho ^)

Hoac mo notebooks/5_deberta_nli_baseline.ipynb:
  1) Restart kernel
  2) Chay cell Setup
  3) (Tuy chon) chay cac gate
  4) Chay cell Train

Ket qua:
  - models/answer_filter/run_1/
  - results/deberta_nli/summary_classification_report.csv
  - results/deberta_nli/summary.json
  - results/deberta_nli/nli_zeroshot/

Danh gia luon dung data/labeled_merged_test.csv (tap test dong bang).
Train/val lay tu data/labeled_merged.csv sau khi loai cac mau test.

Chi chay lai NLI (bo qua train DeBERTa):

    python scripts/run_deberta_nli_baseline.py ^
      --config configs/experiments/filter_training.yaml ^
      --skip-train --skip-overfit-gate

3. RAGAS FILTER (phuong phap chinh)
-----------------------------------
Xem notebooks:
  - 3.1_ragas-feature-extraction.ipynb
  - 3.2_filter-training.ipynb

Hoac START_HERE.md / README.md.

4. KIEM TRA DON VI
------------------
    python -m pytest tests/test_data_split.py tests/test_imports.py tests/test_filter_metrics.py -v

5. GHI CHU DANH GIA
-------------------
- Nguong DeBERTa chon tren val bang min-FPR @ recall >= 0.70
- Khong dung argmax tai 0.5 lam quyet dinh cuoi
- Bang ket qua: dataset,num_samples,accepted,acceptance_rate,accuracy,precision,recall,f1,roc_auc

Chi tiet: docs/DEBERTA_NLI_BASELINE.md , docs/EVALUATION.md , docs/DATA_AND_SPLITS.md
